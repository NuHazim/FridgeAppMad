WIA 2007 - MOBILE APP DEVELOPMENT PROJECT 

TITLE APP: FridgeBuddy
- FridgeBuddy is a smart mobile app that helps you track and manage the items in your fridge.
- It monitors inventory, reminds you of upcoming expiry dates, and suggests ways to use food before it goes bad.
- Helping you reduce food waste, save money, and keep your fridge organized.
